Our story

A small tan building on a rural road, packed every Friday night.

Five years ago we opened the doors with a short menu, a couple of crepe pans, and a habit of knowing every regular by name. Not much has changed. The menu's grown a little. The pans are well-seasoned. And we still know your usual order.

What we do is simple - real ingredients, cooked from scratch, served warm. Crepes folded to order, eggs cracked the minute you walk in, soup simmered all morning. The kind of breakfast and lunch you'd make at home if you had the time, and the kind of Friday dinner that makes the drive worth it.
